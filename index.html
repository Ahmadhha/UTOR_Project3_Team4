Data Set Descriptions


  • Diet Composition Database

    • Diet compositions by macronutrient

      • The average per capita supply of calories derived from carbohydrates, protein and fat, all measured in kilocalories per person per day.

    • Diet compositions by food groups

      • Average per capita dietary energy supply by commodity groups, measured in kilocalories per person per day.

  • Burden of disease Databse

    • Burden of disease by cause

      • Total disease burden, measured in Disability-Adjusted Life Years (DALYs) by sub-category of disease or injury.

    • Share of total disease burden by cause

      • Total disease burden, measured in Disability-Adjusted Life Years (DALYs) by sub-category of disease or injury.

    *Note: Disability-Adjusted Life Years (DALYs) measure the total burden of disease - one DALY equals one lost year of healthy life.

Data Set Limitations


  • Diet Composition Database

    • Data limited to Year 2013

    • Overall country information only available

  • Burden of disease Databse

    • DALYs does not differentiate between unlhealthy life and death by disease

    • DALYs is only an estimation
